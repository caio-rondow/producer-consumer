==================================
    Producer-Consumer Problem
==================================

Test Cases:

> The first line is the buffer size
N>0

> In the next lines, indicate with
'p' with you want to produce and
'c' with you want to consume.

> In the final line, put a '.' to
finish the input.

----------------------------------

EX:

4
p
c
.

----------------------------------

Compile and Execute:

> Compile: g++ main.cpp LimBuffer.cpp -lpthread
> Execute: ./a.out < input_file_here.txt
